FEDEX RATE INCREASE ANALYSIS - JANUARY 2026
================================================================================

CONFIRMED: FedEx implemented a systematic base rate increase
            effective January 1, 2026

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

RATE CARD CHANGES:
  - 138 of 140 rate cards increased (98.6%)
  - 100% of changes were increases (zero decreases)
  - Average increase: 5.93%
  - Range: 4.49% to 6.71%
  - Average dollar increase: $1.66

ACTUAL SHIPMENT IMPACT:
  - December 2025: 50,272 shipments @ $24.72 avg base
  - January 2026:   1,646 shipments @ $30.37 avg base
  - Change: +22.83% average, +35.83% median

WHY THIS IS A RATE INCREASE (NOT NATURAL VARIATION):
  1. FedEx uses deterministic pricing (no natural variation)
  2. Standard deviation within rate cards = $0.00 in Dec 2025
  3. 98.6% of rate cards changed (too systematic to be random)
  4. 100% of changes were increases (no decreases)
  5. Tight clustering around 5.9% (deliberate percentage increase)
  6. Effective date: exactly January 1, 2026
