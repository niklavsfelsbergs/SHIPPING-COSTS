## 7. Important Warnings

1. **Term 2 (Jun 23, 2026+):** All surcharge discounts EXPIRE and revert to published list amounts
2. **Earned Discounts:** Do NOT apply to special zones (9, 14, 17, 22, 23, 25, 92, 96) except Ground Economy
3. **Volume Surge:** If international weekly weight increases >15% from prior 12-week average, FedEx can refuse shipments or modify pricing
4. **Early Termination Events:**
   - Terminating agreement early
   - Transitioning 20%+ volume to competitor
   - Soliciting bids >120 days before term end
   - Failing to meet $5M minimum revenue
